## ‚ö†Ô∏è Common Pitfalls to Avoid

1. **Do NOT:**
   - Make financial performance guarantees
   - Promise specific investment returns
   - Give personalized financial advice
   - Recommend stocks/investments as professional advisor
   - Use aggressive sales language ("Make $5000 overnight")

2. **DO:**
   - Use educational language ("Learn about...")
   - Include disclaimers on financial content
   - Recommend consulting professionals
   - Provide balanced perspectives
   - Cite sources for claims

3. **Banner Ads:**
   - Do NOT use misleading banner ads
   - Do NOT place ads on disclaimer pages
   - Do NOT use "Click here" style deceptive ads
   - Clearly label ad spaces
