## ‚ùì FAQ

**Q: Won't deleting posts hurt my traffic?**
A: Your traffic is already minimal (Google isn't indexing low-value content). Deleting duplicates helps Google understand your unique content better. Keep URLs by redirecting old posts to the new consolidated version.

**Q: How long until Google re-reviews?**
A: Usually 1-2 weeks, but can take up to 30 days. Be patient - they often re-review multiple times before final decision.

**Q: What if they reject again?**
A: Ask for specific feedback via AdSense support. Common issues: "Not enough content" (need 20+ quality posts), "Site design" (improve UX), "No author info" (add bio).

**Q: Can I publish new posts while waiting?**
A: Yes, but they should be high-quality and follow the guidelines above. Avoid the old template approach.

**Q: Should I publish duplicate posts under different slugs?**
A: No, that makes it worse. Google's algorithm specifically penalizes duplicate content. Consolidation is the right move.
