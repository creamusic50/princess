When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information about your device, including:

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

We may use third-party analytics services to help understand how visitors use our website. These services may collect information such as your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on pages.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.
